Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Mayor Excelsior LLC (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you visit our website, contact us, or request a quote for cleaning services.

Information we collect

We may collect:

  • Contact and quote details you submit through forms or messages (for example, name, phone, email, address or service area, and details about the cleaning you need).
  • Communications you send by email, WhatsApp, or similar channels when you reach out to book or ask questions.

How we use information

We use this information to:

  • Respond to quote requests and schedule or perform cleaning services.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us operate our business (for example, website hosting, email, or form delivery tools such as Formspree), only as needed for those services.

Retention

We keep information only as long as needed to provide services, meet legal obligations, or resolve disputes, unless a longer period is required by law.

Your choices

You may contact us to ask questions, update information, or request deletion of certain data we hold, subject to legal exceptions. Email: mayorexcelsiorllc@gmail.com.

Children

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect the latest version.
